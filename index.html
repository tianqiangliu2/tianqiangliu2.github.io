Resume

I am currently working at Google on Street View.

From 2010 to 2015, I was a PhD student in Graphics Group at Princeton University. I did research on computer graphics, particularly analysis and synthesis of 3D shapes and scenes, advised by Professor Thomas Funkhouser. I defended my thesis on July 23rd, 2015.

I received my B.Eng. from Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University in 2010. Before that, I was in Yao class at Tsinghua University, which was founded and directed by Andrew Chi-Chih Yao.

I did undergraduate research in Graphics and Geometric Computing Group, where I was advised by Professor Shi-Min Hu.
